Sweep Synthesizer

TR-2099

This is a synthesizer focused on sample creation. I use it to create drums and FX.

I describe the frequencies I want, the waveforms I want and what will happen during a time period. Then I add effects in my DAW, layer the samples I create here with real drum samples and so until I get my sound right. It is also dope for Hip Hop hats. And transitions. And noise.
